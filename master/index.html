ABOUT US

City Choice Oilwell (CCOW) Ltd is an independent Nigerian Oil & Gas Company. We are the leading Diesel (AGO) supplier and distributor of major energy/petroleum products and we cater to a wide range of industries which include but not limited to financial sectors, Banking, Real Estate, Government, Lifestyle, Media, Construction, Communication, Manufacturing, Education, Residence, Restaurants, Entertainment, etc. We provide a reliable, efficient and safe means to purchase quality petroleum products at the lowest possible prices.

We are a Customer Oriented High Quality Diesel Fuel Supplier committed to keeping our customers satisfied with our premium diesel delivery networks. We do not only bring exceptional diesel supply services to your doorstep but we also serve you with quality products at competitive pricing.
